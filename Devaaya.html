Philosophy


Mercure Goa Devaaya Retreat is a luxury Retreat Resort situated in the pristine island of Divar (just 10 kms. away from the capital city Panaji). Divar is a picturesque and romantic island far away from the maddening crowd, surrounded by the mystic River Mandovi, green fields with abundance of fishing activities happening in the river and its tributaries.Divar Island Village per se is unlike other villages in India. It is spotlessly clean, dotted with superb Portuguese Era memories, peaceful atmosphere that is very rare to find in today’s world. Once you are in Devaaya you forget everything and concentrate on your holiday and wellness.


Devaaya is an unique Retreat Resort for both wellness and leisure. You can easily rewind there in the simplicity of the 6 acres property with lush gardens, fruit trees and with such facilities that are not found in other Hotels and Resorts – jogging tracks, village walks, cycling, boat rides, swimming, large pavilion for yoga sessions and authentic wellness facilities which are hard to find in this modern scenario.Devaaya will take you to an altogether new world of peace and tranquility. We have guided tours by our own staff to show case the island, its beautiful Churches atop the hill, over looking the River Mandovi and its tributaries, the village with all its shops and taverns will make you absolutely enchanted.
